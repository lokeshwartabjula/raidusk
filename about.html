The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) is created with an intention of nurturing entrepreneurship skills of the students. The cell provides a strong platform to pursue entrepreneur activities for budding entrepreneurs by organizing entrepreneurship motivation camps, entrepreneurship awareness camps, and entrepreneurship development programmes. The EDC of the College has collaborated with the Entrepreneurship Development Institute of Tamil Nadu, to enhance entrepreneurial skills among the students and to provide the right orientation, training and counseling for the students.

Objectives.

To create awareness on entrepreneurship among students through camps & training programmes

To identify & motivate budding entrepreneurs by creating entrepreneur culture.

To provide information on entrepreneurial opportunities.

To collaborate industry and R&D cell activities of the college.

Nurturing entrepreneurship skills of the students.
